# Meta Ads Mastery

Comprehensive Meta (Facebook/Instagram) advertising knowledge base. Covers algorithm fundamentals through advanced scaling strategies.

## Two-Campaign Framework (Source of Truth)

**Credit:** Tony Yu (The Fortune Cookie Show)

1. **Testing (ABO)** — ad-set-level budget, one creative angle per ad set, kill losers fast → `meta-ads-campaigns-testing-abo.md`
2. **Scaling (CBO)** — campaign-level budget, duplicate proven winners from ABO, let Meta optimize → `meta-ads-campaigns-scaling-cbo.md`

**Key rules:** ABO for testing (you control budget per creative). CBO for scaling (Meta optimizes among winners). Never add untested creative directly to CBO.

## 2026 Reality

- **Creative is the new targeting** — 70-80% of performance from creative quality
- **Broad targeting beats micro-targeting** — Meta's AI outperforms audience hypotheses
- **Advantage+ is the default** — manual campaigns for niche situations only
- **Video dominates** — Reels/short-form outperforms static by 50%+
- **CAPI is mandatory** — pixel-only tracking misses 30-50% of conversions

What still works: PAS copywriting, customer testimonials, retargeting warm audiences, first-party data, the two-campaign structure.
